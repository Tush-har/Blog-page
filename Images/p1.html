Verdict

Accurately priced OnePlus Nord 2 is one of a kind device to invest in. Buyers can trust their choice with detailed images, great visuals and prolonged usage hours. Also, OnePlus has collaborated with MediaTek in this particular device to produce the best performing chipset MediaTek Dimensity-AI. 

Experience power packed smartness to the fullest
Display and Camera

OnePlus Nord 2 comes with a 6.43-inch AMOLED type display featuring a screen resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels. The bezel-less display of the smartphone carries a 20:9 aspect ratio, a pixel density of 409ppi besides Corning Gorilla Glass v5 coating. 

The smartphone carries a Triple-Camera configuration on its rear face. With a 50MP Primary Camera on the lead followed by an 8MP Ultra Wide Angle Camera, a 2MP Depth Shooter. ISO control, Continuos Shooting, Exposure compensation, LED Flash, Digital Zoom, Face Detection etc are some of the features flaunted by the rear camera setup. Additionally, there is a 32MP Primary Camera placed on the front.

Configuration and Battery

OnePlus Nord 2 gets the latest 5G companion MediaTek Dimensity 1200-AI chipset with 8GB RAM. To offer a super-fast operating experience, the smartphone is also equipped with an Octa-core (Quad-core, 2.6GHz, Cortex A78 + Quad-core, 2GHz, Cortex A55) processor powered by a Mali-G77 MC9 GPU.  

The smartphone is provided with a 4500mAh Li-Polymer type battery, which is non-removable by nature and is also compatible with 30W Warp Charging technology. 

Storage and Connectivity 

OnePlus Nord 2 receives an internal memory of 128GB non-expandable storage space. The device supports a host of connectivity options like Bluetooth v5.1, A-GPS Glonass, Mobile Hotspot, Wi-Fi, Type-C USB charging, 5G & 4G VoLTE network, etc.
